3. What are stocks? ━━━━━━━━━━━━

Stocks represent a portion of a company that has been exchanged in return for an amount money. Over time, although the size of that chunk of company remains the same, the value, or the cost, of that chunk changes- either decreases or increases. This chunk of company's called a stock.

2. What are savings? ━━━━━━━━━━━━

Saving is collecting a sum of money over a period of time without spending. This is done to achieve an end goal, such as buying a car or a house.
